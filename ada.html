PROJECT
OVERVIEW

About

Aesthetic Design Alliance is a landing website for a construction and home improvement company located in Manhattan, NY. The website serves as the online face of the ADA company, contains contact information, and showcases renovation projects.

Key Features

  • Designed and developed the company's website from inception, including creating the logo, defining the color palette, and establishing a cohesive brand identity.
  • Used Figma to create the initial design to present the client with a clear and compelling vision.
  • Led the website development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, resulting in the successful launch of a fully functional website.
  • Conducting ongoing website maintenance and updates, ensuring content accuracy, visual consistency, and alignment with the company's objectives whenever needed.

Timeline

The entirety of the project took two weeks to complete in July 2024. Research and the initial Figma design process took three days; the development, being the most time-consuming part of the project, took nine days; and finally, debugging, website hosting, and final touch-ups took roughly two days.

PROCESS

Research

To create a user-friendly and visually compelling website, I researched how users interact with home improvement service sites, focusing on companies in the New York City area and comparing them with those in other states. This helped me identify common user expectations, trust-building features, and effective design patterns. I analyzed service layouts, project galleries, and contact flows to determine what drives engagement and credibility.

Design Process

The design process began with creating initial mockups in Figma to align with the client on the overall vision and layout of the website from the start.

I established a cohesive visual identity by defining the brand’s color palette and designing a custom log that reflected the company’s focus on clean, modern aesthetics. These elements laid the groundwork for a consistent and professional look across the entire site.

Development Process

The development phase involved building the website from the ground up using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to ensure a responsive and visually polished experience across devices. Once development was complete, I hosted the site on the client's custom domain using GitHub Pages, enabling easy access, version control, and streamlined updates.

OUTCOMES

Challenges

One of the initial challenges was aligning with the client’s vision for the logo, which required several redesigns. After multiple rounds of feedback and refinement, we were able to successfully capture the brand’s identity.

Additionally, I encountered technical issues while setting up the website on the custom domain. Debugging involved troubleshooting terminal errors and researching deployment issues, which ultimately led to a successful resolution and a smooth hosting experience.

Current State

The result was a fast-loading, fully functional website that effectively represents the Aesthetic Design Alliance brand online. I continue to do website maintenance and updates for the Aesthetic Design Alliance whenever needed.
